Preparation & Serving

macOS

  1. Download the Chickenwing.dmg installer.
  2. Open the DMG and drag Chickenwing to your Applications.
  3. Launch the app; you will be prompted for your password to enable network capturing.

Windows

  1. Download the Chickenwing-Setup.exe installer.
  2. Run the installer. Windows will show a UAC Prompt—click "Yes" to grant Administrator access.
  3. This access is required for the Npcap driver to capture raw network packets.
